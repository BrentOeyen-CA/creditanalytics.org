About Credit Analytics

Credit Analytics is a technology and service provider offering expertise in quantitative modelling and data analysis within the area of credit. Furthermore, it offers fully developed software solutions and will develop tailored solutions meeting specific advanced analytics requests.

Our specialists are industry experts who have worked with both leading and smaller financial institutions. Their expertise and experience can help your organisation defining its needs to meet tomorrow's expectations.

With our automated decision model solutions we can quickly answer challenging questions, such as "does the organization have sufficient data to build IRB models or to model IFRS 9 provisions", that have significant implications. In addition, other manual decision processing such as fund or stock selections can be automated and backtested.
